How to make avocado cream

On hot, sunny days, having an avocado ice cream cone is wonderful. Today, Group-7 will show you how to make this ice cream! Let's get into the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 100ml condensed milk
  • 150ml heavy cream
  • Sugar, vanilla extract
Step 1. Prepare the avocado mixture

Peel and pit the avocados. Blend the avocado flesh, coconut milk, condensed milk, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract in a blender until smooth.

Step 2. Make the ice cream base

- Pour 150ml of heavy cream and 30g of sugar into a mixing bowl. Use an electric mixer to whip the cream until soft peaks form.
- Fold the whipped cream into the avocado mixture until well combined.
- Transfer the avocado cream mixture into a container and freeze it for at least 6 hours.

Step 3. Optional toppings

You can serve the avocado ice cream with chopped nuts, chocolate syrup, or fresh fruit on top. Be creative with your toppings!

Step 4. Enjoy!

Once the avocado ice cream is fully frozen, scoop it into bowls or cones, add your favorite toppings, and enjoy!
