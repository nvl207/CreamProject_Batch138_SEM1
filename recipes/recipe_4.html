How to make strawberry cream

On hot, sunny days, having a strawberry ice cream cone is delightful. Today, Group-7 will show you how to make this ice cream! Let's get into the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 100g jackfruit pulp
  • 100ml coconut milk
  • 100ml condensed milk
  • 150ml whipping cream
  • Sugar, peanuts, vanilla
Step 1. Prepare the jackfruit mixture

Mix 100g jackfruit pulp, 100ml coconut milk, 100ml condensed milk, and 1 teaspoon vanilla in a bowl.

Step 2. Make the ice cream base

- Pour 150ml whipping cream and 30g sugar into a plastic cup, then use an electric mixer to whip the cream.
- When the cream is spongy, pour it into the bowl of jackfruit mixture and continue mixing well.
- When the mixture is mixed together, put it in a jar and store it in the freezer for 5-6 hours.

Step 3. Roast peanuts

Put the peanuts in the pan, add a little salt and roast over low heat until the peanut shells turn golden, then turn off the heat and peel.

Step 4. Decorate

After the ice cream has had enough time to freeze, take it out of the refrigerator and put it in an ice cream cup, adding some
peanuts and jackfruit chunks on top.
You can drizzle a little chocolate or eat with waffles. Store the remaining ice cream in the freezer to eat later.
