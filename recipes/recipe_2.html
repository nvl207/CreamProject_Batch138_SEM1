How to make mango cream

On hot, sunny days, having a mango ice cream cone is wonderful. Today, we'll show you how to make this delightful treat! Let's get into the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 200g ripe mangoes, chopped
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 100ml condensed milk
  • Sugar, nuts (optional)
Step 1. Make sponge cake base

- Put in a bowl 40g of unsweetened fresh milk, 40g of corn oil, 18g of sugar and stir until the sugar dissolves evenly. Sift into
the bowl, add 55g low gluten flour, 6g corn starch and continue stirring the mixture. Add 3 more egg yolks, stir well.

- Put the egg whites in a bowl and use a mixer to beat them well for about 30 seconds. Use 30g of sugar divided into 3 equal
parts and add each part in turn, beating until smooth, until the mixture is loose, spongy and has sharp peaks when lifted.

- Mix the egg white mixture into the yolk powder evenly, then pour it into the cake mold and pat gently to remove any air
bubbles. Bake the cake at 145 degrees Celsius for about 55 - 60 minutes. After baking, take the cake out of the oven and cut
off the edges of the cake. Then, you cut the cake into 3 equal layers.

Step 2. Make frosting

Put 400g of fresh cream and 30g of powdered sugar in a bowl, use a whisk to mix well, then place the bowl of ice cream in another
bowl of ice water to prevent the cream from melting.

Step 3. Prepare mango

After buying mangos, wash and peel them. Cut half of the mango you brought into thin slices for decoration, and half cut into cubes.

Step 4. Decorate

- You put the cake layer at the bottom, apply a layer of frosting on top and place the diced mango on top, then brush another
layer of cream on top of the mango. Do the same with the second cake layer.
- When placing the third layer of cake, cover the top and around the cake with cream, then arrange thin pieces of mango on the cake to form
a flower shape. Choose the smallest slice, fold it into a circle and place it in the innermost layer, then roll the remaining mango slices slightly
and arrange them on top.

Step 5. Finished product!
